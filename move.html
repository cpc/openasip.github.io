The MOVE Project

MOVE is the previous codesign toolset for TTA-based systems, which is now deprecated. We think of MOVE as a prototype and inspiration for OpenASIP. It brought the TTA idea to reality the first time.

You might want to try out MOVE. Beware though, the code has suffered quite a lot of "bit rot" during the years and it might be hard to get it to compile and working robustly in new Linux distributions. We do not provide support for the MOVE tools anymore so you are on your own.
